Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Spoolio is a 3D-printer filament tracker built and operated by Qaizo Studio ("we", "our", "us"). This policy explains what we collect when you use the Spoolio mobile app, how we use it, and how you can remove it.

Summary

  • We collect the minimum needed to run the app: your sign-in email and the spool data you choose to enter.
  • Your spool data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore + Cloud Storage), accessible only to your account.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers.
  • You can delete your account and all your data from inside the app at any time.

What we collect

DataWhyWhere
Email address Identify your account so your spools sync across devices. Firebase Authentication
Display name (Google / Apple Sign-In only) Personalize the greeting on the home screen. Firebase Authentication
Spool data: brand, material, weight, temperatures, color, photo, notes Track your filament inventory — the core function of the app. Firestore (per-user collection)
Spool photos Visual identification of each spool. You choose to take or upload them. Firebase Cloud Storage (per-user)
Language preference, view-mode preference Remember your settings between launches. Local device storage (AsyncStorage)

What we do not collect

  • No location data.
  • No device contacts.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Facebook SDK, etc.).
  • No background tracking — the app only acts when you open it.

How we use it

Strictly to provide the app's features:

  • Authenticate you, so your data syncs only to your account.
  • Store and retrieve the spools you enter.
  • Display photos you attach to spools.
  • Send password-reset emails when you ask for them.

Photos and camera

The app uses your device camera and photo library only when you tap the camera button to add a spool photo or scan a label. Photos you take never leave your device unless you save the spool — at which point the photo is uploaded to your private Firebase Storage area, accessible only by you. The label-scan feature uses on-device OCR (Google ML Kit) and does not upload the label photo anywhere; it is discarded after the form is filled.

Third parties

Spoolio relies on Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage), Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, and Sentry (crash reporting) to provide its features. These sub-processors handle your data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase — privacy notice
  • Google Sign-In — privacy notice
  • Apple Sign-In — privacy notice
  • Sentry — crash reports only. When Spoolio crashes, we send Sentry a stack trace, the app version, the device model, the OS version, and a breadcrumb trail of the actions leading up to the crash. We do not send your spool data, your photos, or your email address. privacy notice

We do not share your data with anyone else.

How long we keep it

Your data is kept as long as your account exists. When you delete the account from inside the app (Settings → Account → Delete account) we remove all your spools, usage history, photos, and authentication record. This is irreversible.

Your rights

  • Access — you can see all your data inside the app.
  • Correction — edit any spool from its detail screen.
  • Deletion — Settings → Account → Delete account removes everything we hold about you.
  • Portability — email support@qaizo.app if you need an export of your data.

Children

Spoolio is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact support@qaizo.app and we'll delete it.

Security

Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS. Firestore security rules enforce that only your own authenticated account can read or write your spool data. We do not have direct access to your password — Firebase Authentication stores it as a salted hash.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy in a way that affects what we collect or how we use it, we'll change the "Last updated" date at the top. Substantive changes will also be communicated in-app on the next launch.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: support@qaizo.app
