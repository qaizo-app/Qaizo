Qaizo combines simplicity with powerful tools to give you complete control over your personal finances.

Qaizo AI

Chat with your personal AI financial advisor. Ask questions about spending, get savings tips, analyze trends — all based on your real data. Your data stays private.

Voice & Smart Input

Say "coffee 5 dollars" or type naturally. AI understands amount, category and account. The fastest way to log expenses.

Receipt Scanner

Snap a photo of any receipt. AI reads amount, store, date and items. Multi-photo for long receipts. Duplicate protection.

Smart Budgeting

"Free Money Today" calculator. Set budgets by category with visual progress bars. Daily spending limits that adapt.

Advanced Analytics

Balance history, cash flow charts, expense breakdown, period selector (7D-1Y). Three tabs: Overview, Expenses, Trends.

Smart Shopping List

Price history from scanned receipts. Alerts for overdue items. Build lists and share via WhatsApp. Find the cheapest store.

Savings Goals

Create goals with target amounts and dates. Track deposits, see monthly needed amount. Reach your financial dreams.

Recurring Payments

Set up automatic tracking for rent, subscriptions, salaries. Contract end reminders 30 days in advance.

Achievements & Streaks

6 levels from Beginner to Legend. 30-day activity calendar. Dashboard widgets that unlock as you use the app.
